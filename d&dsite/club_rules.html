General Etiquette

-Follow the DM’s lead. Refrain from arguing.

-Don’t hog the spotlight.

-Avoid cross talk & irrelevant conversations.

-Discourage bullying or shaming others.

Table Etiquette

-Only make rolls with the DMs direction and after declaring an action, not before.

-Roll dice on the table where results are visible.

-Re-roll dice that land askew or roll off the table.

-Don’t look up monster stats or enemy spells.

-If you and the DM disagree on rules, the DM’s interpretation is the rule for the session. In other words “DM’s word is law.

Character Requirements

-New characters here start at 1st Level.Once one of your characters here reaches 7th level, you can start creating new characters at 3rd level instead.

-Use the Standard Array (15,14,13,12,10,8), or the Point-Buy method to determine ability scores.No rolling for them.

-Your character can use content from official Wizards of the Coast 5e sources only. DMs can add or remove content allowed at their tables.

-Add 5 to your character’s maximum hit points.

-Use the equipment from your class and background. No rolling for starting wealth.

-To keep the game a positive and cooperative experience, your character should not be Chaotic Evil

-You cannot play characters of the opposite sex (school rule not ours)
