Mission Statement

The goal of the BYU Idaho D&D Club is to create a space where those who wish to play Dungeons and Dragons can do so in a welcoming and fun environment. A sense of belonging and community is integral to the enjoyment of everyone involved, so belittling, bullying, discrimination, or any other form of offensive behavior will not be tolerated. Without a spirit of good sportsmanship and teamwork the entire purpose of playing together is destroyed. Core values of the club include creativity, inclusivity, respect, and good humor. We are all here to have fun, so anything that detracts from that, whether it be through the inappropriate actions of others or an unwelcoming environment, must be dealt with in order to maintain good times for all.
