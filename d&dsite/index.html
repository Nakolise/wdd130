Dungeons and Dragons Club

Make Friends, Create Your Character, and Have an Unforgettable Adventure

Mission Statement

State the club's mission statement, outlining its core objectives and goals. For example, it might be to create a welcoming space for students to enjoy D&D and build a sense of community. Explain the core values that guide the club, such as inclusivity, creativity, respect, and fair play. Highlight how these values are central to the club's activities. "Everyone is responsible for making sure that everyone else has an enjoyable time. If anyone feels offended, belittled, or bullied by the actions of another person, the entire purpose of getting together to play is defeated,"

Introduction

Begin with a warm and inviting introduction to your club. Explain its role on the college campus and how it contributes to the college community. Provide a brief overview of what D&D is for those who might not be familiar with the game, emphasizing its role as a collaborative tabletop role-playing game.
